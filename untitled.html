Agile transformation and Business Agility

The goal of an agile transformation is business agility, which is the ability of a business to better respond to change. Sometimes companies can get clogged in the weeds of agile rituals, like believing "stand-ups mean agile", losing sight of the bigger picture. This talk aims to candidly explain the risks and rewards of an agile transformation, showing that it's all about people first, not processes.
